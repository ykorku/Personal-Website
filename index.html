About Me

I am Yunus Korku. I am Turkish.


My name is Yunus, and I am originally from Turkiye. Currently, I am a student at Jacksonville State University, where I am pursuing a major in Computer Information Systems. I have been living in the US for 6 years now, and
I currently reside in a dorm on campus. In my free time, I enjoy watching soccer games and shows, particularly adventure and action genres.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also passionate about sports, especially soccer. I love playing soccer in my free time
and often participate in intramural leagues at my university. I have been playing soccer since I was a child and have developed
a deep appreciation for the teamwork, skill, and excitement that the sport entails.

Aside from sports, I also enjoy watching shows and movies. I am particularly drawn to adventure and action genres as they offer an
adrenaline rush and keep me on the edge of my seat. I often find myself immersed in captivating storylines, eagerly anticipating the
next twist or turn in the plot.
